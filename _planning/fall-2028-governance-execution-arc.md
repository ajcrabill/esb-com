## Arc Overview

The Fall 2028 Governance Execution Arc is the board's **second full governance execution cycle** — the first cycle was Spring 2028 (Mar–Jun 2028). The Summer 2028 Governance Renewal arc (Jul–Sep 2028) gave the board a "protect-apply-add" framework for refining its governance practices before the fall.

Where the Fall 2027 arc taught boards how to run a governance cycle for the first time, the **Fall 2028 arc assumes a board that has completed a full cycle, completed a summer renewal, and is now executing governance with experience.**

The framing: *This board is not learning governance from scratch. It is advancing its governance practice into its second year of execution.*

### November Sub-Arc — Goal Trajectory & Mid-Year Direction (4 posts)

**Theme:** November is where the board shifts from executing the fall data review to setting direction for the spring cycle. Goal trajectory, budget direction, and superintendent partnership are the three threads.
